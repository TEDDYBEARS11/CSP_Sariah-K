PARK CITY

WHAT IS PARK CITY KNOWN FOR?

Park City, Utah, is a renowned mountain resort town known for its ski slopes and outdoor recreation, particularly the Deer Valley Resort and Park City Mountain Resort. Located east of Salt Lake City, it's a popular destination for both winter and summer visitors. Park City is famous for its world-class skiing and snowboarding, with two major resorts and a vast network of trails. Park City Mountain Resort is the largest ski resort in the United States. They are also well known for hosting the sundance festival.
